The Polytechnic University of Amozoc

It is a Public Institution that belongs to the General Coordination of Technological and Polytechnic Universities. Which seeks to respond to the social needs of training professionals in a comprehensive manner, equipping them with the necessary skills to integrate into any work environment. The educational offer of this University favors learning through real situations.
