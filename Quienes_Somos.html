ABOUT US?

The Polytechnic University of Amozoc is a public institution of higher education committed to the development of the municipality that houses it, the State that supports it and the country that supports it. Seeks to form intact human beings, through the acquired skills are participants in the knowledge society.

Based on values ​​such as respect and honesty, this house of studies works day by day to achieve its objectives, offering the university community quality services, cutting-edge technological infrastructure and professionalism.

Inserted in the rewarding challenge of Higher Education and assuming the responsibility that this entails. The Polytechnic University of Amozoc is an excellent option to achieve goals and contribute to the development of all those linked to our community.
